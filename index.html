WHAT WE OFFER

AME Portraits has photographers for all specialties. Click to see portfolio samples.

Maternity

Celebrate the incredible journey of motherhood with a beautiful maternity session. We focus on capturing the glow, the connection, and the anticipation of this special time, providing you with timeless and tender portraits that you and your child will cherish forever.

Newborn

Preserve the fleeting moments of your baby’s first weeks with a gentle and artistic newborn session. Handled with the utmost care and patience, we create stunning, serene portraits of your little one, focusing on their tiny details and the pure love of your new family.

Toddler

Capture your child's vibrant personality and boundless energy with a fun and engaging toddler photoshoot. From genuine smiles and curious expressions to playful movement, we document this exciting stage of growth in a natural and authentic way that truly reflects their unique spirit.

Cake Smash

Mark your child’s first birthday milestone with a joyous and messy cake smash session! These fun-filled photoshoots are all about laughter, sweet indulgence, and unforgettable expressions, resulting in hilarious and adorable images that perfectly capture this once-in-a-lifetime celebration.

Prewedding/Wedding

Kick off your wedding story with a personalized pre-wedding (or engagement) session that captures your unique chemistry and excitement. Whether it’s in a location special to you or a stunning scenic backdrop, we'll create relaxed, beautiful, and romantic images for a perfect save-the-date or a timeless memory.
